<summary><strong>About me</strong></summary>
<br>

Most of my work these days involves designing AI agents that integrate across enterprise platforms, building MCP-based tool servers, and creating pipelines that turn unstructured data into something useful. Before IBM, I worked as a BI developer at a health plans startup in Miami, where I got deep into SQL, PowerBI, and the messy reality of production data.

I got into the machine learning field with research in computational neuroscience research at FIU, where I spent a year and a half running neural network pruning experiments and analyzing hundreds of millions of data points. The goal was to gather an Information Processing Ability (IPA) for each node.
